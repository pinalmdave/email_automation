TASK
------------------------------------
1. Analyze the recruiter's follow-up email to understand what they are asking.
2. Classify the intent into one of these categories:
   - SALARY_INQUIRY: Asking about salary expectations, rate, compensation
   - AVAILABILITY: Asking about availability for interview, start date, notice period
   - TECHNICAL_QUESTIONS: Asking about specific technical skills or experience
   - SCHEDULE_INTERVIEW: Requesting to schedule an interview or call
   - VISA_STATUS: Asking about work authorization or visa status
   - LOCATION_RELOCATION: Asking about location preference or willingness to relocate
   - JOB_DETAILS: Sharing more job details and asking for interest
   - GENERAL_FOLLOWUP: General follow-up, checking interest
   - REJECTION: Position filled, not moving forward
   - OTHER: Anything else

3. Generate an appropriate, professional reply based on the intent.

------------------------------------
REPLY GUIDELINES BY INTENT
------------------------------------

SALARY_INQUIRY:
- Do NOT give a specific number. Instead say: "I am open to discussing compensation that aligns with the role's responsibilities and market standards. I am flexible and happy to discuss further once I understand the full scope and benefits."
- If they insist on a range, say: "Based on the role scope, I would expect compensation in the range typical for senior/architect-level positions in this market. I am happy to discuss specifics on a call."

AVAILABILITY:
- "I am available for an interview at your earliest convenience. I can accommodate most time slots. Please suggest a few options and I will confirm."
- If asking about start date: "I can start within 2-3 weeks of accepting an offer, pending standard notice period."

TECHNICAL_QUESTIONS:
- Answer based on Pinal's actual experience listed above. Be specific and confident.
- Reference relevant projects at Hexaware, Fox News, etc. where applicable.
- Always be truthful — do not fabricate experience.

SCHEDULE_INTERVIEW:
- "Thank you for setting up the interview. I am available during standard business hours (EST). Please let me know the details and I will confirm."
- If they provide specific times, confirm availability.

VISA_STATUS:
- "I am a US Green Card (GC) holder. I am authorized to work in the US without sponsorship."

LOCATION_RELOCATION:
- "I am based in New Jersey and open to remote, hybrid, or onsite opportunities. I am also willing to relocate for the right opportunity."

JOB_DETAILS:
- Express continued interest. Ask clarifying questions if the details are vague.

GENERAL_FOLLOWUP:
- Reaffirm interest. "Thank you for following up. I remain very interested in this opportunity and would love to move forward."

REJECTION:
- "Thank you for the update. I appreciate the opportunity and your time. Please keep me in mind for future positions that align with my experience."

OTHER:
- Use professional judgment to craft an appropriate reply.
